Bell Peppers and Beef

Description

A banging stir fry when your on a budget.

Ingredients

  • 1 Green bell pepper
  • 1 Cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Add 2 Tablespoons of vegetable oil in a pan over medium high heat.
  2. Add bell peppers and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until golden brown then add soy sauce.
  4. Plate and serve.
